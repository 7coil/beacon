## Rules
Violating the following rules may cause you to recieve actions taken against you.

1. Do not be a dick, and please be respectful of other people
	- Offence | Action
	  ------- | -----------
		1       | Warning
		2       | Muted (4 Hours)
		3       | Kicked
		4       | Banned
2. Use the correct channel for your conversations
	- Offence | Action             | Notes
	  ------- | ------------------ | -----
		1       | Warning            |
		2       | Muted (1 Hour)     |
		3       | Muted (8-24 Hours) | Length is up to the member of staff processing you
3. Do not spam or troll
	- Offence | Action     
	  ------- | -----------
		1       | Warning
		2       | Muted
		3       | Kicked
		4       | Banned
4. Do not have excessive background noise
	- Try using _Push to Talk_
	- Offence | Action                           | Notes
	  ------- | -------------------------------- | ------
		1       | Warning                          |
		2       | Given **Push to Talk Only** role | To prove your background noise has been eliminated and remove the role, please find a member of staff to enter into a voice chat with to prove you have rectified the issue.
5. Do not advertise
	- Offence | Action
	  ------- | -----------
		1       | Banned
6. Do not abuse pings
	- Offence | Action
		------- | -----------
		1       | Warning
		2       | Kicked
		3       | Banned
7. Do not post racist content
	- Offence | Action
	  ------- | -----------
		1       | Banned
8. Do not post NSFW content
	- Discord Partner Guidelines (we're trying to be one) prohibits NSFW content in servers.
	- Offence | Action
	  ------- | -----------
		1       | Banned

This list is non-exhaustive - Moderators reserve the right to take action for any reason, including but not limited to:
- Protecting the members of the community
- Keeping the server in order

Staff have the final say:
- If you choose to involve staff in your disputes, we have the final say
- Try blocking users first
	- If a user continues, contact a Moderator or Administrator
